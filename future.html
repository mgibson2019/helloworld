My Future Plans

I have a lot of different things I want to do! After finishing the Neumont web certificate, I plan on pursuing a degree in sound design and engineering. I also am a president for Chronicle Theatre Company, a non-profit theatre company that specializes in producing shows from undiscovered playwrites. I want to continue to expand our shows to include the shows that have a wonderful message but may not be the most popular. I want to share the love of theatre that I was taught.
